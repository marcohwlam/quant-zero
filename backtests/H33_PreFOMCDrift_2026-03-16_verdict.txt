ROOT CAUSE ANALYSIS
=============================================================================

The H33 strategy is based on the Lucca & Moench (2015) finding that ~80% of
the equity risk premium accrues in the 24h before FOMC announcements. However:

1. STRATEGY DECAY: The pre-FOMC drift documented in academic literature appears
   to have decayed significantly post-2012. IS performance is dominated by the
   2007-2010 GFC period when the effect was strongest.

2. SHY FILTER INACTIVE: The SHY momentum filter (designed to block rate-hike
   environments) never triggered in 2007-2021. This means the "filtered" variant
   is identical to "unfiltered" — the filter provided zero value in-sample.

3. OOS FAILURE: Near-zero OOS Sharpe (0.02) over 2022-2025 confirms strategy
   decay. Post-2015 the pre-FOMC drift has been widely documented and likely
   arbitraged away.

4. HIGH WF VARIANCE: Walk-forward OOS Sharpes range from -2.26 to +2.06, 
   indicating the strategy has no consistent edge — pure randomness with
   very small samples (4 trades per window).
